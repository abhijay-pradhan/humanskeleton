Ribcage(25 Bone)

Sternum + 12 Pairs Of Ribs. Types Of Ribs:

1) 7 Pairs Of Ribs Are Attached To the Sternum.
2) All Ribs Are Attached To The Thoracic Vertebrae.
Name Pair Of Ribs
True Ribs 1-7
False Ribs 8-10
Floating Ribs 11-12
Note: True Ribs Are Attached To Sternum & Vertebral Column. False Ribs Are Attached To True Ribs & Vertebral Column. Floating Ribs Are Attached To Vertebral Column
